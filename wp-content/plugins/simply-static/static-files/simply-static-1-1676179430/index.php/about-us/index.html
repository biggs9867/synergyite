About Us

Introduction

Welcome to Synergy Engineering Consultant & Construction, a leading company in the field of construction and engineering services in Nepal. We specialize in providing a full range of services, including municipality drawing, AutoCAD 2D & 3D, supervision, estimation, valuation, and structure analysis. Our team of experienced and skilled architects, engineers, and construction professionals work together to deliver high-quality projects that exceed client expectations.At Synergy Engineering Consultant & Construction, we are dedicated to providing top-notch construction services to our clients. Our team of experienced architects, engineers, and contractors work together to bring your vision to life.

Expertise

Our team of experts has years of experience in the construction industry and has the knowledge and skills to handle any project, big or small. We use the latest technologies and techniques to ensure that each project is completed efficiently and effectively. Our team is dedicated to providing the best quality service at an affordable rate to our clients.

Supervision

At Synergy Engineering Consultant & Construction, we believe in direct supervision to ensure that each project is completed to the highest standard. Our supervisors are on-site at all times to monitor progress and ensure that all work is carried out according to plans and specifications. This direct supervision helps to minimize any potential problems and ensures that each project is completed on time and within budget.

 

Prevention of Damage

We understand that construction projects can often result in damage to the surrounding environment and community. That’s why we take a proactive approach to minimize any potential damage and ensure that each project is completed with care and sensitivity. Our team takes all necessary measures to protect the environment and surrounding community, including implementing protective measures and taking measures to prevent soil erosion.

Consultant

At Synergy Engineering Consultant & Construction, we believe in providing the best quality service to our clients. That’s why we offer free consultation services to help clients understand the various aspects of their project. Our team of experts is available to provide expert advice and guidance on all aspects of construction, from design and planning to execution and completion.

 

Complete Solution

We offer a complete solution for all construction projects, including the supply of all necessary materials and equipment. Our team works closely with clients to ensure that their vision is realized and that each project is completed to the highest standard. We take a hands-on approach to each project and are dedicated to ensuring that each client is completely satisfied with the final result.

 

Commercial and Residential:

At Synergy Engineering Consultant & Construction, we offer our services for both commercial and residential projects. Our team has extensive experience in designing and constructing buildings of all sizes and types, from small homes to large commercial developments. No matter the size or scope of your project, we have the expertise to deliver a high-quality solution that exceeds your expectations.
